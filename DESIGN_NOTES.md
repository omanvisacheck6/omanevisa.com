# Oman e-Visa Portal - Design Reference

## Key Observations from Official Portal

### Header Section
- Royal Oman Police emblem (left side)
- Arabic text "شرطة عمان السلطانية" (Royal Oman Police in Arabic)
- "ROYAL OMAN POLICE" text below emblem
- Oman e-Visa logo with pink/magenta "e" and blue swoosh (center-right)
- Arabic text below e-Visa logo
- Navigation breadcrumb: "Know your Visa Type | Track Your Application | Share Your Experience | Contact Us | Frequently Asked Questions | Login | العربية"

### Navigation Bar
- Blue background (#2E5FA3 or similar)
- White text for navigation items
- Green highlight boxes for active/important sections
- Arabic language toggle on the right

### Main Content Area
- Light gray/white background
- Centered content card with subtle shadow
- Green circular checkmark icon (✓) indicating "Visa is Active"

### Visa Details Card
- White background with rounded corners
- Centered layout with generous whitespace
- Visa information displayed in label-value pairs
- Bold font for values (visa number, names, etc.)
- Light gray text for labels

### Decorative Footer Pattern
- Blue background (#2E5FA3)
- White decorative pattern (traditional Omani geometric design)
- Footer links: Copyright, Privacy Policy, Disclaimer, Sitemap, Terms and Conditions

### Color Palette
- Primary Blue: #2E5FA3 (navigation, footer)
- Accent Green: #26A65B (checkmark, status indicator)
- Pink/Magenta: For e-Visa logo
- White: Main background
- Light Gray: Secondary backgrounds and text

### Typography
- Sans-serif font (likely Segoe UI or similar)
- Bold for headings and important values
- Regular weight for labels and body text
- Supports both English and Arabic text

## Design Philosophy
- Official government portal aesthetic
- Clean, professional, and trustworthy
- Clear information hierarchy
- Accessible and easy to navigate
- Responsive design for mobile devices
